Visualisation Tips:

Consider starting a journaling routine to record your visualisation experiences.
Journaling can help you notice patterns and changes in your thoughts and emotions.
- Visualise a future version of yourself and the positive changes you want to make. 
- Practice gratitude by visualising things you are thankful for in detail. 
- Imagine yourself achieving your goals and experiencing success. 
- Visualise a protective shield of positive energy around you. 
- Choose a quiet and comfortable space for your visualisation practice.
